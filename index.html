About SpaceX

SpaceX was founded in 2002 with the goal of making space travel more affordable and paving the way for human exploration of Mars.

With reusable rockets like Falcon 9 and powerful spacecraft such as Dragon, SpaceX has achieved numerous milestones, including being the first privately funded company to send a spacecraft to the International Space Station (ISS).

Explore Our Missions

SpaceX's missions range from satellite deployment and resupplying the ISS to paving the way for human exploration of Mars. Each mission is a step closer to interplanetary travel.
