Falcon Heavy: Power and Innovation

Unmatched Power

Falcon Heavy is the most powerful operational rocket, capable of lifting massive payloads into space. It is designed for heavy missions to low Earth orbit, the Moon, and Mars.

Achievements

Since its first launch in 2018, Falcon Heavy has demonstrated its reliability and capability. Notably, it carried Elon Musk's Tesla Roadster into space.

Future Missions

With its impressive payload capacity, Falcon Heavy is vital for future missions to deep space, including crewed missions and planetary exploration.
