Dragon Spacecraft

The Dragon spacecraft is SpaceX's revolutionary capsule designed for both cargo and crewed missions.

About Dragon

The Dragon spacecraft was developed to carry supplies to the International Space Station (ISS) and beyond. It became the first privately developed spacecraft to dock with the ISS in 2012.

Dragon's latest iteration, Crew Dragon, is designed to safely transport astronauts, marking a new era in commercial spaceflight. Its advanced systems and reusable design make it a vital part of SpaceX's long-term goals.

Key Features

  • Reusable spacecraft design
  • State-of-the-art life support systems
  • Autonomous docking capabilities
  • Capacity for up to 7 astronauts
