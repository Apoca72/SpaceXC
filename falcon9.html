Overview

Falcon 9 is the first orbital-class reusable rocket designed by SpaceX. It is capable of delivering cargo and crew to orbit efficiently.

Falcon 9 works seamlessly with the Dragon spacecraft for delivering cargo to the International Space Station.

Missions

Falcon 9 has completed over 200 successful missions, including satellite launches and ISS resupply missions.

For larger payloads, consider exploring Falcon Heavy.

Technology

Equipped with innovative reusability technology, Falcon 9 reduces the cost of space access significantly.

Discover more about SpaceX's reusability innovations that enable cost-efficient missions.
